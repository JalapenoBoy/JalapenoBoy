You can reach us via Twitter and on our official Discord. You either tweet to us directly @chilicollection or to one of our team.

We'll announce the minting price soon! Stay tuned - you will first find it out on our Discord.

ChiliCollection is going to bring real value to the NFT/DeFi Space. Because of that we'll be catering to a large and wide audience, thus it's absolutely crucial to launch on a widely spread and safe blockchain.
Our developer team has chosen the Ethereum Mainnet as a perfect match for those requirements.
